How It Works

Contract Manufacturing Process

From initial brief to delivered product — a standard OEM/ODM engagement follows these steps.

1

Product Brief

You provide the target product type, performance requirements, packaging format, and budget range.

2

Formulation Match

We propose existing base formulations or develop a custom formulation to your specification.

3

Sample Approval

Lab samples produced for your evaluation. Iterate until the product meets your performance and sensory targets.

4

Production & Packaging

Full-scale production with your approved packaging — private label, co-branded, or generic. QC release per agreed specification.

Available Product Categories

Standard base formulations are available for all categories. Custom formulation, fragrance, color, enzyme systems, and packaging are configured to your requirements.

Detergent Powder / Washing Powder

Laundry Powder · High-Foam / Low-Foam Options
Base Formulation

Standard laundry powder with balanced surfactant system. Active content configured to your target — typical ranges from 12–20% active matter for economy grades to 25%+ for premium formulations.

Customization Options

Fragrance (customer spec) Speckles (red / blue / green) Enzyme systems Optical brighteners Oxygen bleach

Packaging

100g sachets to 1,000kg bulk bags. Private-label bags, pouches, cartons, and buckets.

Detergent Liquid / Washing Liquid

Liquid Laundry Detergent · HE Compatible
Base Formulation

Liquid laundry detergent with surfactant blend (anionic + nonionic). Standard and high-efficiency (HE) compatible formulations available. Typical active matter range 15–30% depending on positioning.

Customization Options

Color (customer spec) Fragrance (customer spec) Viscosity profile Enzyme systems Optical brighteners

Packaging

500mL to 5L bottles, pouches, and bulk totes. Private-label bottle design and labeling.

Dishwashing Liquid

Hand Dishwashing Liquid · Manual Dishwash
Base Formulation

High-foaming hand dishwashing liquid with anionic surfactant system (LABSA neutralized + SLES + betaine or amide foam booster). Balanced for grease-cutting performance and skin mildness.

Customization Options

Color (customer spec) Fragrance (customer spec) Viscosity / concentration Antibacterial additives Moisturizing agents

Packaging

500mL to 5L bottles, pouches, and bulk totes. Private-label bottle design and labeling.

SDS and product specification available upon request.

Laundry Detergent Pods

Unit-Dose Laundry · Laundry Capsules
Base Formulation

Unit-dose laundry detergent in water-soluble PVA film. Concentrated liquid formulation — single pod per wash. Standard, color-safe, and multi-chamber (detergent + softener + stain remover) configurations available.

Customization Options

Chamber count (1–3) Fragrance (customer spec) Film type / dissolution profile Pod size / fill weight

Packaging

Child-resistant pouches, tubs, and cartons. Private-label packaging design. Custom pack counts.

SDS and product specification available upon request.
